Tribhuwan_University

The BSc CSIT (Bachelor of Science in Computer Science and Information Technology) course offered by Tribhuvan University is a four-year program divided into eight semesters. The course curriculum is designed to provide students with a strong foundation in computer science and information technology, as well as advanced skills in specific areas of specialization.

First Two Years

Completing the first two years of a BSc CSIT program can provide students with a range of benefits, including foundational knowledge, skill development, exploration of interests, preparation for advanced coursework, and transferable skills. These benefits can help students succeed both academically and professionally.
